# Contributing

Thanks for your interest in improving `lf-hermes-memory-addon`.

## How to contribute
- Visit **LegionForge.org** for project information and updates.
- Email **info@legionforge.org** to discuss contributions, fixes, and collaboration.

## Before submitting changes
- Keep the skill spec-compliant.
- Avoid machine-specific paths, IP addresses, usernames, tokens, or private repo names.
- Keep `SKILL.md` focused and move detailed material into `references/`, `templates/`, or `scripts/`.
